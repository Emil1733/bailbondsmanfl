# Programmatic Local SEO: Internal Linking & Cannibalization Strategy

To maximize the search authority (PageRank) flow across **Bond Florida** and prevent search engines from confusing our pages (keyword cannibalization), we must implement a strict **Siloed Hub-and-Spoke** linking hierarchy.

---

## 1. Page Hierarchies & Query Mapping (Cannibalization Prevention)

Keyword cannibalization occurs when Google cannot determine which page on a site is the most relevant for a given search query, resulting in pages competing against each other and dragging down overall rankings. 

We prevent this by mapping specific search intent to specific page types:

| Page Level | Target URL Structure | Primary Target Keyword Pattern | Secondary Intent Keywords |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 1: Home** | `/` | `Florida Bail Bonds`, `Statewide Bail Bonds FL` | `Online bail bonds florida`, `fast bail fl` |
| **Level 2: Directory** | `/locations` | `Florida Bail Bonds Directory` | `Florida county jails`, `florida service areas` |
| **Level 3: County** | `/county/[county]` | `Bail Bonds [County Name] County` | `[County] jail booking`, `[County] inmate release` |
| **Level 4: City** | `/county/[county]/[city]` | `Bail Bonds [City Name] FL` | `[City] police department bail`, `bondsman [city]` |
| **Level 5: Service** | `/services/[service]/[city]` | `[Service Name] Bail Bonds [City Name]` | `DUI bail [city]`, `traffic warrant [city] bond` |

### ⛔ The Golden Rule of Anchor Text:
*   **Never** link to a city page using the generic anchor text "Bail Bonds" or "Florida Bail Bonds." That anchor text belongs *strictly* to the Homepage.
*   When pointing to a city page, the anchor text **must** include the city qualifier: `"Bail Bonds Cape Coral"` or `"Cape Coral Bail Bondsmen"`.
*   When pointing to a county page, the anchor text **must** include the county qualifier: `"Hillsborough County Jail Details"` or `"Bail Bonds in Orange County"`.

## 2. Internal Linking Rules (PageRank Funneling)

To pass authority effectively through the site and ensure Google's bot stays within logical context silos, we use three distinct routing paths:

### A. Vertical Downward Linkage (Discovery)
*   **Homepage** links to `/locations` (Main Navigation Header & Footer).
*   `/locations` links to every **County Hub Page** and groups the **City Landing Pages** underneath them.
*   This acts as the primary "crawl pipeline" ensuring that every single page on the site is a maximum of **2 clicks away** from the homepage, boosting Googlebot’s crawl budget efficiency.

### B. Vertical Upward Linkage (Context & Trust)
*   Every **City Page** must link back to its parent **County Page** (e.g., Cape Coral linking back to Lee County).
*   Every **Service x City Page** must link back to its parent **City Page** and **Service Hub Page**.
*   **Implementation**: This is handled programmatically via breadcrumbs and contextual links:
    *   *Example on Cape Coral city page*: "All arrests are processed at the [Lee County Jail (Core Facility)](/county/lee)..."

### C. Horizontal Linking (Neighborhood Authority Sharing)
To prevent pages from becoming dead-ends and to distribute authority among adjacent cities, we implement **Horizontal Sister-City Links** at the bottom of the templates.
*   A city page must link to other city pages *within the same county* (e.g., Cape Coral links to Fort Myers; Fort Myers links to Cape Coral).
*   **The Constraint**: A city page in Lee County should **never** link horizontally to a city page in Miami-Dade County. This preserves the strict geographical Silo. If PageRank leaks across unrelated silos, Google's entity matching becomes confused.

## 3. Step 2 & 3 Technical Roadmap

### 1. Homepage & Locations Hub FAQ Schema (Step 2)
We will add structured JSON-LD FAQ schema to the homepage and the locations page. 
*   **Home Page Schema**: Targets statewide queries (`how much does a bail bond cost in Florida?`, `can you do bail online in Florida?`).
*   **Locations Page Schema**: Targets directory queries (`where are Florida inmates held?`, `how do I search for a Florida jail address?`).

### 2. City-Level Interlinking (Step 2)
We will write a utility that dynamically generates "Nearby Service Areas" for cities based on the parent county. Since Cape Coral is in Lee County, it will automatically link to Fort Myers at the bottom of the page, and Fort Myers will link to Cape Coral.

### 3. Mobile Sticky Call CTA (Step 3)
We will create a lightweight, performant React component (`MobileStickyCTA.tsx`) that renders a permanent sticky bar at the bottom of the screen *only* on mobile viewports. It will trigger a direct `tel:` protocol link to the agency phone number, maximizing conversion rate on our high-impression mobile traffic.
